What is Portfolio?

The word portfolio has multiple meanings, including a collection of work, a financial term, and an online newspaper:


Collection of work

A portfolio is a compilation of work that demonstrates your skills, education, and experience. It can include a variety of materials, such as photos, designs, articles, and project templates. Portfolios can be used by students to showcase their accomplishments over a period of time. They can also be used by professionals to demonstrate their skills and qualifications.


Financial term

A portfolio is a collection of investments, such as stocks, bonds, and real estate. The goal of a portfolio is to balance risk and reward by spreading investments across different types of assets. This practice is called diversification.


Online newspaper

Portfolio.hu is an online financial newspaper in Hungary that focuses on business, economic, and financial news.
